How to use this with Airflow
Because of the argparse setup at the bottom, when you eventually build your Airflow DAGs, you won't need to write 9 different Python scripts. You can use the exact same file and just change the argument in your SparkSubmitOperator.

For example, your Airflow task for products would look something like this under the hood:
spark-submit /opt/airflow/etl_pipeline/silver_transformations.py --table products

And your task for orders would simply be:
spark-submit /opt/airflow/etl_pipeline/silver_transformations.py --table orders
